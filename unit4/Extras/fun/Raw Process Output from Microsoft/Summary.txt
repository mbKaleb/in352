Inside the MinidumpMiscInfo section, the ProcessorCurrentIdleState value was 1, indicating the CPU was idle at the moment of capture. This lines up with what I'd expect during a deadlock: both threads are blocked waiting to acquire a lock, not actively executing instructions, so the processor has nothing to do and simply sits idle rather than spinning or consuming cycles.
